A frontend mockup of the two remaining changes so you can react before I build them into the game: Study Cards (concept + drug flashcards, no question re-quiz) and Extras & navigation.

Study Cards become a flashcard tool for two things only: concept definitions and drug study. Re-answering every case is removed — missed questions stay in the end-of-rotation review.

Tap the card to flip. Concepts show definition, mechanism, and key points; drugs show class, MOA, use, and what to watch. Study, not a quiz — no scoring.

Break room, Faceoff, and Patient journeys move out of the hidden “More” dropdown onto one clear Extras shelf, each with a stated purpose. Every screen also gets a consistent ← Back and an app-level Settings gear (top bar).
