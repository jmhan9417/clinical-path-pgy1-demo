# Original music library

All 22 tracks in this folder were composed and supplied by Minseok "Joey" Han for The Clinical Path on 2026-07-21. Do not replace the source files in the user's Downloads folder; these are project copies.

## Scene routing

- Title: lighter breeze, forest, classroom, and short theme variants
- Roadmap: open afternoon, walking, forest, and classroom variants
- Orientation: classroom, walk, and short linked-poem variants
- Acute care: short memory, season, and walking variants
- Critical care / Floor Rounds: both Neon Wave variants, Neon Radio Whisper, and Stay With Me Tonight
- ID / Oncology: long theme, season, and linked-poem variants
- Ambulatory care: breeze, long walk, and forest variants
- Practice advancement: long memory, classroom, and linked-poem variants
- Leadership: long season, theme, and Stay With Me Tonight
- Patient stories: short/long main theme, linked poem, and memory variants
- Review: short forest, breeze, walk, and classroom variants
- Finale: Stay With Me Tonight, long season/memory themes, and the longest afternoon-walk variant

Tracks are streamed one at a time, rotate within each playlist, and crossfade when the scene changes. Dialogue scenes use lower music gain than Title, Roadmap, and Floor Rounds.
